## Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Account

1. Visit the AMBF Connect website
2. Click **"Register"** or **"Sign Up"**
3. Fill in your details:
   - **Full Name** - Your display name
   - **Email** - For login and notifications
   - **Password** - Must be at least 6 characters
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. Check your email for verification (if required)

### 2. Complete Your Profile

After registration, complete your profile to unlock all features:

1. Click on **"Profile"** in the bottom navigation
2. Add the following information:
   - **Profile Photo** - Hover over the photo area and click the camera icon
   - **Job Title** - Your current position
   - **Company** - Your organization
   - **Bio** - A brief description about yourself

**ğŸ’¡ Tip:** A complete profile (100%) helps other attendees connect with you more easily!

### 3. Join an Event

1. Go to **"Events"** from the home screen
2. Browse available events
3. Click on an event to see details
4. Click **"Join Event"** to register
5. You'll receive a confirmation and be added as an **Attendee**

### 4. Install the App (Optional)

For the best experience, install AMBF Connect as an app on your device:

#### On Mobile (iOS/Android):
1. Open the website in your browser (Safari for iOS, Chrome for Android)
2. Look for the **"Install"** banner that appears at the top or bottom of the screen
3. Tap **"Install"** or **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. The app icon will appear on your home screen
5. Launch it like any other app!

#### On Desktop:
1. Look for the install banner at the top of the page
2. Click **"Install"** when prompted
3. The app will open in its own window
4. Access it from your taskbar or applications folder

Stay updated with event announcements and messages:

## Need Help?

### Common Questions

**Q: I haven't received my verification email**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Wait a few minutes for delivery
- Try registering again with the same email

**Q: I can't see the dashboard**
- The dashboard is only for event **Organizers** and **Admins**
- Regular attendees use the Events and Networking tabs

**Q: How do I change my role?**
- Contact the event organizer
- Organizers can change your role to Speaker, Media, etc.

**Q: The app isn't working offline**
- Some features require internet connection
- Install the PWA for better offline support

**Q: How do I leave an event?**
- Currently managed by organizers
- Contact the event admin if you need to be removed
